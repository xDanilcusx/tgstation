10 March 2021

ATH1909 updated:

  • Using amputation shears on incapacitated, critted, dead, unconscious, paralyzed, staminacritted, etc. targets is now much faster. Jittering still penalizes the speed at which you can cut someone's limbs off with amputation shears, and that penalty now applies even if the target is stunned or in critical condition.

MTandi updated:

  • Nanite Monitoring now works with pinpointers and Lifeline
  • Pinpointers now track properly on multi-z stations
  • Stationary crew monitor now shows data from all levels of multi-z station
  • Nanite Dermal Button icon set now contains more icons, but without color options

Son-of-Space updated:

  • The beer nuke is now available on Kilo, all is as it should be. Go find it!

SparkezelPL updated:

  • Now all emp proof cells are emp proof

Useroth updated:

  • proc/reset_pull_offsets() resets the mob's offsets to their own base_pixel_x and base_pixel_x, instead of the puller's

06 February 2021

Couls updated:

  • Restored gorillification from irradiated monkeys

Fikou updated:

  • syndie gasmasks are small
  • fixes galoshes description
  • makes blast cannons not seem like an innocent item before a bomb is loaded
  • renames metal sheets to iron sheets

Jared-Fogle updated:

  • The ninja's energy katana now uses right click to dash instead of being a toggle.

KathrinBailey updated:

  • Chameleon masks and neck slot items fit into boxes and pockets, like their counterparts they're camouflaging as almost always do.

Kylerace updated:

  • turrets made via the holodeck laser guns now stop working when the guns get deleted
  • radio uplinks can no longer become unusable due to the code frequency
  • autolathes can no longer create items for less materials than they can be recycled for

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Reinforced plasma windows now fully block superconduction, or heat movement
  • Heat from a delaminating supermatter won't leak out of the chamber on its own now, things will be a bit more spicy now.

MTandi updated:

  • Fixed NTOS File Manager renaming/deleting wrong programs when used with external disc

Melbert, sprites by Cybersaber101/blueshirtguy updated:

  • Two new plants! Roses and Carbon roses can now be grown in botany. You can even make rose bouquets!
  • Four new plant traits! Exothermic Activity in Ghost Chili Peppers, Endothermic Activity in Chilly Peppers, Auto-Juicing Composition in Pineapples, and Hallucinatory Feedback in Laughing Peas.
  • Three new plant genes! Welding Fuel in Combustible Lemons, Nitrogen in Coffee Arabica, and Formaldehyde in Corpseflowers.
  • The Plant Analyzer was re-written to unify output formatting.
  • A bunch of botany related things now use defines for their minimums and maximums.
  • Plants now almost entirely use the setter/adjuster procs to change their stats instead of doing it directly. Planted, spreading Growshrooms how have a minimum endurance of 10 instead of 0 as a consequence but they're functionally almost identical.
  • Plant analyzers now show what graft you would get from a plant or seed, as well as the yield modifier a tray has.
  • Plant analyzers now have a better description and examine text to show it as other modes you can switch it to.
  • Plant analyzers now show the stats of grafts when you scan them in addition to their plant gene.
  • Inedible grown items now gain their chemicals properly after being harvested
  • Inedible grown items now give out a proper analyzer readout on both modes
  • Replica pods now give out the actual DNA they have in them instead of 'sampleDNA' on analyzing.
  • Rose sprites, Carbon Rose sprites, and a Rose Bouquet courtesy of Cybersaber101/blueshirtguy

Pepsiman0 updated:

  • fixes the tend wounds healing the wrong damage.

Qustinnus updated:

  • You no longer need to hold shift to open the context menu as a ghost
  • You can no longer kidnap ghosts with a moving table
  • Combat mode prevents you from being pushed by movement
  • hauntium is now properly applied to the photo.
  • you can no longer shoot and melee with guns at the same time

Qustinnus, Actionninja & Goof for some audio, fikou for beeping twice updated:

  • Adds station traits: Small modifiers that can randomly be chosen each round. They are usually logged in the intercept the station receives early in the round
  • Announcement audio is now stored in a datum to make it easy to add different announcers

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Friendly commendations now track by the recipient's ckey rather than the mob they controlled at the time of commending. You'll still have to use their character name when specifying them.
  • Trying to commend an invalid target as an admin will give you proper feedback that it did not go through
  • The config entry for friendly commendations has been changed from 'COMMENDATION' to 'COMMENDATION_PERCENT_POLL'
  • You can now remove embedded objects in a patient by using a hemostat on the patient's affected limb, no surgery necessary!
  • Removed the now obsolete "remove embedded objects" surgery
  • Objects thrown at high speed (like by a hulk or amped up disposal outlet) are now more likely to embed, scaling with the speed they're flung into you at
  • Most bullets are now slightly more likely to embed
  • .357 bullets are now somewhat more likely to cause piercing wounds

Skoglol updated:

  • Removes the adrenal implant and the adrenal nanite program.

  • The civilian remote now properly affects service department doors
  • Some areas are now properly affected by ai monitoring

  • Heretic's void cloak is once again visible on examining a player when they have unequipped the hood direct from the equipment menu.
