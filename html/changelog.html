  • Changes the carrying traits on Latex/Nitrile gloves into a skillchip.
  • Medical doctors start the round with a quick carry skillchip (was the latex glove trait).
  • Paramedics and the CMO start the round with a quicker carry skillchip (was the nitrile glove trait).
